Rock ,Paper and Scissors Game

In this game The winning hand beats the losing hand as follows

  • Rock beats Scissors (because rock can crush scissors)
  • Scissors beats Paper (because scissors can cut paper)
  • Paper beats Rock (because paper can cover rock)

If both players choose the same shape, the game is a tie.
